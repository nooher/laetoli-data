# Laetoli — Sovereign Technology for East Africa
**One-page executive summary · Laetoli Ltd · 2026**

> *Uhuru wa kidijitali kwa Afrika Mashariki — lugha yetu, data yetu, vijana wetu.*
> Digital sovereignty for East Africa — our language, our data, our youth.

---

### What
An **open, Swahili-first sovereign technology stack** with three connected parts:

- **SNIL** — the first African-origin programming language: Kiswahili grammar,
  keywords, and errors; runs in-browser and compiles to Python and JavaScript;
  includes a built-in course (DARASA), REPL, and VS Code extension.
- **Laetoli Data** — a self-hostable sovereign backend (PostgreSQL + PostgREST +
  lean auth + a near drop-in Supabase-subset SDK) that runs on a Tanzanian VPS or a
  **Raspberry Pi** — "school in a box," offline-capable.
- **Education** — curriculum that teaches young Kiswahili speakers to build real
  software in their own language.

### Why
Two dependencies exclude most Africans from building technology: a **foreign-language
barrier** (every mainstream language assumes English) and a **foreign-SaaS dependency**
(national data hosted abroad, billed in dollars, per project, forever). Laetoli's
answer: **own the language, own the data, teach the next generation** — open and
affordable. Aligned to SDGs 4, 8, 9, and 10.

### Proof (shipped, not slideware)
- **SNIL — live:** https://snil.vercel.app · repo `nooher/snil` · engine
  `@laetoli/snil` (`nooher/snil-core`) · Apache-2.0.
- **Laetoli Data — verified end-to-end**, deployable via Docker on a VPS or Pi;
  Apache-2.0, with deployment and Raspberry Pi guides.
- **Delivery track record:** government-grade Swahili-first platforms already built
  by the team — **THOS** (health interoperability), **TISEZA** (SEZ digital twin),
  **TANROADS** (road-reserve assets), **IRMP** (IP rights) — plus **Tiba** health
  apps and **Kasuku** (African literature & streaming). Umbrella: **laetoli.tz**.
  *(Government integrations on these platforms are in deployment / deferred to
  procurement; presented as proof of capacity.)*

### Model
**Open core** (everything Apache-2.0) sustained by **support & hosting**,
**education** (DARASA, teacher training, certification, Pi school-in-a-box kits),
and **integration/solutions** for national deployments.
